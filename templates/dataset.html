Column Explanation

  • Age = The person’s age
  • Workclass = Type of work (Federal Gov, Unemployed, etc)
  • Final Weight = The number of units in the target population that the responding unit represents
  • Education = Types of unit's education
  • Years of Education = number of years of education in total, which is a continuous representation of the discrete variable education
  • Marital Status = describe the unit’s relationship with significant other
  • Occupation = type of job
  • Role in Family = the unit’s role in the family
  • Race = the unit’s race
  • Gender = the unit’s gender
  • Capital Gain = income from investment sources other than wage/salary
  • Capital Loss = loss from investment sources other than wage/salary
  • Workhours per Week = unit’s amount of work time per week
  • Native Country = unit’s origin country
  • Income = unit’s income (target variable)
